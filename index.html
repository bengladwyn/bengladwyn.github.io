About Us

Nowhere is the experimental verification of a scientific theory more challenging than in particle physics, requiring immensely sensitive detectors and robust data analysis and processing. We've found the recent advancements in machine learning to analyze enormous data sets and produce such high-precision results to be truly transformative.

So too have we found the application of data science techniques in neutrino physics to be groundbreaking. The intricacies of neutrino interactions and the vast amounts of data generated in neutrino experiments benefit immensely from cutting-edge data science methods, enabling us to extract meaningful insights from the subtle signals buried within the noise.
